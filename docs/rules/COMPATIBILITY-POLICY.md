# Edition Compatibility Policy

Forge & Fable currently exposes only strict 2014 rules in production. Strict 2024 research is isolated until its data, choices, validation, and tests are approved.

`2024-with-legacy-options` is intentionally not implemented. Legacy subclasses, feats, species, and backgrounds must not be mixed into strict 2024 characters by default. Any future compatibility support must use an explicit, source-backed matrix and an intentional conversion or compatibility choice.

Existing characters without an edition field are migrated to `2014` because that is the edition represented by the existing production rules data. No character is silently converted to 2024.

Artificer is an explicit exception to the twelve-class 2024 core inventory: the licensed Tasha’s Artificer is researched as `artificer-2014`, while 2024 Artificer material is deferred until a specific official source is approved. Unearthed Arcana is not treated as production content.
