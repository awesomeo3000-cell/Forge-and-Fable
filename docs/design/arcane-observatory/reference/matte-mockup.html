The Brass Meridian

Do not turn the inner and outer rings together. The eastern ring answers to starlight. The western ring answers to a spoken name.

When the furnace is awake, the meridian does not point north. It points toward whatever is listening.

  • Begin with the eastern ring
  • Speak the keeper’s name once
  • Do not answer the second voice
