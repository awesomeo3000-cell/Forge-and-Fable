# Commission screen plate art — subjects and prompts

Date: 2026-07-15. Author: Fable. Companion to `backdrop-prompts.md` and
`portrait-prompts.md` (same shared style rules: matte, painterly, no
glow/bloom, no text).

The commission ("Commission a character") mockups in
`mockups/commission-mockups.html` currently use engraved SVG placeholder
plates drawn inline (`#plate-defs` sprite). Those can ship as-is — zero
asset weight, crisp at any size, brass ignites on hover via the shared
ink-wash filter. If painted plates are preferred, generate with the
prompts below and drop them into `public/Start/` under the existing
filenames (`start-standard.jpg`, `start-quick.jpg`,
`start-premade-1..4.jpg`) — the live screen and the mockups both key off
those names.

Shared constraints for all plates: portrait orientation ~900×1080
(3:3.6), export ≤250 KB each. Palette locked to the AO tokens: deep
ink-blue night field (#070d16–#152438), pale slate linework, antique
gold/brass (#b3924a–#d9b967) reserved for the subject. The UI applies a
desaturating ink-wash filter at rest and lifts it on hover/selection —
so the brass must carry the color story, not the sky.

## Plate I — Standard ("the full commission, chapter by chapter")

Subject: the great orrery over an open tome — the whole apparatus of a
life, assembled deliberately.

> matte oil painting, portrait orientation, a grand brass orrery with
> concentric rings suspended in a deep ink-blue night sky, small planets
> on the rings, a warm brass sun at center, beneath it an open
> leather-bound tome on a dark stone sill seen edge-on, an arc of seven
> faint gold stars between orrery and book, engraved hairline quality to
> the ring work, muted palette of deep blues, slate, antique gold, no
> glow, no lens flare, no text, no borders

## Plate II — Quickbuilder ("guided choices for a faster start")

Subject: a comet over the observatory dome — arrival by the fast road.

> matte oil painting, portrait orientation, a single gold comet with a
> long tapering tail streaking diagonally across a deep ink-blue night
> sky, below it the dark silhouette of a stone observatory dome on a
> ridge with its viewing slit open toward the comet, thin crescent moon
> high in the corner, sparse faint stars, muted palette of deep blues,
> slate, antique gold, quiet and swift, no glow, no lens flare, no text

## Plates III.1–4 — Premade ("archetypes awaiting a name")

Four emblem seals, not portraits — the archetypes are anonymous until
named. One engraved brass roundel per plate on the ink field, emblem
centered: 1. a longsword point-up (the vanguard) · 2. an open tome under
a four-pointed gold star (the scholar) · 3. a slim dagger before a
crescent moon (the knife) · 4. a lute (the voice).

> matte oil painting, portrait orientation, an engraved antique brass
> roundel seal centered on a deep ink-blue field, inside the roundel
> [EMBLEM], drawn in fine pale engraved linework with antique gold
> accents, sparse faint stars in the corners, muted palette of deep
> blues, slate, antique gold, heraldic and quiet, no glow, no text
