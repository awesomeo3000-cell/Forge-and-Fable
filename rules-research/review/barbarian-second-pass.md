# Barbarian Pilot Second Pass

Date: 2026-07-12

Both pilot packets were rechecked against the official 2014 Basic Rules/SRD progression and the official 2024 D&D Beyond Basic Rules class table.

## 2014

- Level 3 subclass, level 6/10/14 subclass features, and level 4/8/12/16/19 Ability Score Improvements are present.
- Rage uses scale at levels 1, 3, 6, 12, and 17; the level 20 record uses the unlimited marker.
- Rage damage scales at levels 1, 9, and 16.
- The 2014 record does not include 2024 Weapon Mastery, Brutal Strike, Instinctive Pounce, or Epic Boon.

## 2024

- Level 1 Weapon Mastery, level 3 Primal Knowledge, level 9 Brutal Strike, level 13/17 Improved Brutal Strike, and level 19 Epic Boon are present.
- Rage uses scale at levels 1, 3, 6, 12, and 17; the level 20 record uses the unlimited marker.
- Rage damage scales at levels 1, 9, and 16; Weapon Mastery scales at levels 1, 4, 10, and 16.
- Subclass features are represented at levels 3, 6, 10, and 14.

The packets remain research records only. The production ruleset remains 2014 until the progression engine and strict edition selection are implemented.
