# Sorcerer, Warlock, and Wizard Pilot Second Pass

Date: 2026-07-13

The six arcane packets were checked against the official 2014 Basic Rules class tables and the official 2024 D&D Beyond Basic Rules class tables.

- Sorcerer keeps 2014 known spells and Sorcerous Origin at level 1, while 2024 uses prepared spells, Innate Sorcery, Sorcerer Subclass at level 3, Sorcery Incarnate, and Epic Boon.
- Warlock keeps 2014 Pact Magic, known spells, Eldritch Invocations beginning at level 2, and Pact Boon at level 3. The 2024 packet records prepared spells, level-1 invocations, Magical Cunning, Contact Patron, and the revised Mystic Arcanum progression.
- Wizard keeps the 2014 spellbook formula and Arcane Tradition at level 2. The 2024 packet records Ritual Adept, Scholar, Memorize Spell, Wizard Subclass at level 3, Epic Boon, and the revised prepared-spell table.

The packets remain research records only. The production ruleset remains 2014 until the progression engine and strict edition selection are implemented.
