# Paladin and Ranger Pilot Second Pass

Date: 2026-07-12

Both edition pairs were checked against the official 2014 Basic Rules class tables and the official 2024 D&D Beyond Basic Rules class tables.

## Paladin

- The 2014 packet records Divine Sense, Lay on Hands, Fighting Style, Spellcasting, Divine Smite, Sacred Oath at level 3, Aura progression, Improved Divine Smite, Cleansing Touch, and the 2014 prepared-spell formula.
- The 2024 packet records Weapon Mastery at level 1, Paladin’s Smite, Channel Divinity and Paladin Subclass at level 3, Faithful Steed, Abjure Foes, Radiant Strikes, Restoring Touch, Epic Boon, and the revised prepared-spell table.
- The 2014 and 2024 packets do not share subclass timing or capstone assumptions.

## Ranger

- The 2014 packet records Favored Enemy and Natural Explorer, known spells beginning at level 2, Ranger Archetype at level 3, the 2014 exploration feature improvements, and Foe Slayer.
- The 2024 packet records Favored Enemy and Weapon Mastery at level 1, prepared spells beginning at level 1, Deft Explorer, Roving, Expertise, Relentless Hunter, Nature’s Veil, Precise Hunter, Epic Boon, and Foe Slayer. Hunter’s Mark free-cast uses scale at levels 1, 5, 9, 13, and 17.
- The 2014 known-spell table and 2024 prepared-spell table remain separate data models.

The packets remain research records only. The production ruleset remains 2014 until the progression engine and strict edition selection are implemented.
