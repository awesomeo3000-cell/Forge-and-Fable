# Druid and Fighter Pilot Second Pass

Date: 2026-07-12

Both edition pairs were checked against the official 2014 Basic Rules class tables and the official 2024 D&D Beyond Basic Rules class tables.

## Druid

- The 2014 packet keeps Druidic, Wild Shape, Druid Circle at level 2, the 2014 Wild Shape CR breakpoints, Timeless Body, Beast Spells, and Archdruid.
- The 2024 packet keeps Druidic and Primal Order at level 1, moves Druid Subclass to level 3, adds Wild Companion, Wild Resurgence, Elemental Fury, Improved Elemental Fury, and Epic Boon, and records the revised prepared-spell counts.
- The 2014 Wisdom-modifier-plus-level preparation formula is not used in the 2024 packet.

## Fighter

- The 2014 packet records Martial Archetype at level 3, Action Surge at levels 2 and 17, Indomitable at levels 9, 13, and 17, and Extra Attack at levels 5, 11, and 20.
- The 2024 packet records Fighting Style, Second Wind, Weapon Mastery, Tactical Mind, Tactical Shift, Tactical Master, Studied Attacks, Epic Boon, and the revised Extra Attack progression.
- The 2014 and 2024 packets keep their separate subclass timing, resource breakpoints, and equipment-training boundaries.

The packets remain research records only. The production ruleset remains 2014 until the progression engine and strict edition selection are implemented.
