# Rogue and Monk Pilot Second Pass

Date: 2026-07-12

Both edition pairs were checked against the official 2014 Basic Rules class tables and the official 2024 D&D Beyond Basic Rules class tables.

## Rogue

- The 2014 packet records Roguish Archetype at level 3, additional archetype features at 9, 13, and 17, and the 2014 core feature cadence.
- The 2024 packet records Weapon Mastery, Steady Aim, Cunning Strike, Improved Cunning Strike, Devious Strikes, Epic Boon, and the revised subclass levels.
- Sneak Attack dice are recorded at each actual scaling breakpoint rather than being treated as a new feature at every level.

## Monk

- The 2014 packet records Ki beginning at level 2, Monastic Tradition at level 3, the Ki-point progression, Martial Arts die breakpoints, and Unarmored Movement breakpoints.
- The 2024 packet records Monk’s Focus, Uncanny Metabolism, Deflect Attacks, Empowered Strikes, Deflect Energy, Disciplined Survivor, Perfect Focus, Superior Defense, and Body and Mind.
- Focus and Ki are separate resource IDs; the 2024 packet does not inherit the 2014 feature names or capstone.

The packets remain research records only. The production ruleset remains 2014 until the progression engine and strict edition selection are implemented.
