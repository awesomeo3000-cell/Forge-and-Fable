# Cleric Pilot Second Pass

Date: 2026-07-12

Both Cleric packets were checked against the official 2014 Basic Rules class table and the official 2024 D&D Beyond Basic Rules class table.

## 2014

- Divine Domain is selected at level 1; domain features occur at levels 1, 2, 6, 8, and 17.
- Channel Divinity uses are represented at levels 2, 6, and 18 as 1, 2, and 3 uses.
- Destroy Undead scaling is represented at levels 5, 8, 11, 14, and 17 with CR thresholds 1/2, 1, 2, 3, and 4.
- Ability Score Improvements are represented at levels 4, 8, 12, 16, and 19.

## 2024

- Divine Order is selected at level 1; Cleric Subclass is selected at level 3 with subclass features at levels 6 and 17.
- Channel Divinity uses are represented at levels 2, 6, and 18 as 2, 3, and 4 uses.
- Sear Undead, Blessed Strikes, Improved Blessed Strikes, Divine Intervention, Epic Boon, and Greater Divine Intervention are represented at their revised levels.
- The 2024 packet uses the official prepared-spell counts and does not inherit the 2014 Wisdom-modifier-plus-level formula.

The packets remain research records only. The production ruleset remains 2014 until the progression engine and strict edition selection are implemented.
