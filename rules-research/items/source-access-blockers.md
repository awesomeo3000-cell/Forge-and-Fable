## Accessibility Notes for Specific Source Types

### Print Books (Majority of Sources)
All major hardcover releases are available through:
- **Amazon / major book retailers:** New and used copies
- **D&D Beyond:** Digital versions with full item tooltips and compendium access
- **Local game stores:** New copies for recent releases

No significant access issues for print sources.

### D&D Beyond Compendium Access
D&D Beyond provides the best access for item research:
- Purchased books unlock full compendium content with tooltips
- Items are tagged with source attribution in the database
- The "Magic Items" and "Equipment" pages filterable by source
- Some items from purchased adventures appear only in the adventure's item appendix on D&D Beyond

**Limitation:** D&D Beyond requires purchase of each book to access its compendium. There is no bulk research API. Manual review is required.

### SRD Sources (Free)
- **SRD 5.1:** Available as free PDF from Wizards of the Coast website
- **SRD 5.2:** Available under CC-BY-4.0 from D&D Beyond
- Both are fully accessible with no purchase required
- SRD 5.1 is OGL; SRD 5.2 is CC-BY-4.0 (irrevocable)

### Extra Life Products
- All Extra Life products on DMsGuild/DriveThruRPG are paid products
- Proceeds go to charity (Extra Life / Children's Miracle Network Hospitals)
- Total cost to purchase all item-containing Extra Life products: approximately $45
